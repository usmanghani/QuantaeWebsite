We have built a revolutionary adaptive learning technology solution to fit in the foreign language learning market. Our solution identifies and self-adapts to meet the students unique learning needs, bringing ease and effectiveness of learning a foreign language to an unprecedented level.

 

Problem: Students differ in their learning needs, and a mismatch between teaching strategy and students' learning needs results in frustration, poor performance or even outright rejection of learning the foreign language. This pain is magnified in difficult languages that are distant from English.

Solution for Students (Self-Study): We have designed a subscription-based online Arabic language-learning platform that adapts to a student's unique learning needs:

      Learning styles the platform will identify the learning style of students and teach the student accordingly.

      Capacity to understand and memorize this ensures that fundamentals are understood before they are built upon and that weak concepts are readdressed.

      Objective of learning Arabic different curriculums will be taught to facilitate different objectives.

Solution for Universities/Institutes: The platform can be used as a tool by teachers to help match teaching strategy to student learning needs:

      Curriculum construction/improvement highly detailed and indexed topic lists can be used to design a curriculum from scratch or improve existing curriculums

      Automated assessment and student reports student performance can be assessed through the platform saving teachers time spent on grading. Analytics provided by the platform can be used to identify weak areas and give individual help.

Market: The initial release will target the Arabic language; the estimated total addressable market opportunity for Arabic-language learning in the US is currently $155M, which is expected to reach $350M in the next 5-years, making it the fastest growing language in US. The key reasons include significant growth of interest after 9/11 for the Arabic culture. The next releases will include Spanish, French, and German. The global language learning market represents over $83b, of which $5b is spent in the US.

Competitive Edge: Our product differentiates itself in three ways:

      Innovative adaptive technology existing one-size-fits-all teaching solutions such as Rosetta Stone and university courses do not effectively address student learning needs resulting in poor performance and lack of motivation in learning.

      Low entry and exit barrier through subscription model University courses lock students into a semester. Not all students have the schedule for this.

      Low price Rosetta Stone and university courses have a high cost entry barrier.
